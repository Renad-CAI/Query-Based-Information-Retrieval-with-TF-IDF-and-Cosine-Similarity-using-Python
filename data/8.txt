football, also called association football or soccer, game in which two teams of eleven players, using any part of their bodies except their hands and arms, try to maneuver the ball into the opposing team’s goal. Only the goalkeeper is permitted to handle the ball and may do so only within the penalty area surrounding the goal. The team that scores more goals wins.
The game:
Normal play consists of two teams of eleven players on field (one on offence the other on defence) 
Football is the world’s most popular ball game in numbers of participants and spectators.
